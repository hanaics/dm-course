What is SEO?

Introduction

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is a strategy used to increase the visibility of a website on search engines like Google and Bing.

Key Elements of SEO:
  • Keyword Research: Finding search terms your audience uses.
  • On-Page Optimization: Using keywords in titles, headings, and content.
  • Technical SEO: Site speed, mobile-friendliness, crawlability.
  • Off-Page SEO: Backlinks and authority building.
Tips to Improve Your SEO:
  1. Use short, descriptive URLs.
  2. Create high-quality, original content.
  3. Optimize images with alt text.
  4. Use HTTPS for secure browsing.

Takeaway: SEO is a long-term investment that improves your visibility and traffic over time.
