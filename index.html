Events

Events digital marketing is the strategic use of online tools and platforms to promote and manage events, aiming to engage audiences, build anticipation, and ultimately drive attendance

Lead Generation

In digital marketing, lead generation is the process of identifying and attracting potential customers, typically online, and converting their interest into a lead, which is a prospect who has expressed an interest in a business's product or service

Marketing And Advertisement

Marketing encompasses the broader strategy of understanding customer needs and creating value, while advertising is a specific tactical method used within that strategy to promote products or services through paid channels.

Content Performance

Performance marketing is a digital marketing strategy where advertisers pay only for measurable results like clicks, leads, or sales, focusing on driving tangible outcomes and RO.

Brand Awareness

In digital marketing, brand awareness refers to how familiar customers are with a brand and its products, including their ability to recognize and recall the brand.

Gross-App-Management

Gross-App-Management (GAM) in digital marketing refers to the strategic process of maximizing the number of app downloads and user engagement within a mobile app, often through various digital marketing techniques.

App Installs

In digital marketing, app installs refer to a user successfully downloading and opening a mobile application for the first time.

Video Views

In digital marketing, video views represent the number of times a video is played or watched.

Reach

In digital marketing, reach refers to the number of unique individuals who have seen your content, such as a social media post, ad, or website content, at least once.

Catalogue Sales

In digital marketing, "Catalogue Sales" refers to a sales strategy where businesses use a product catalogue, whether printed or digital, to showcase and sell their products or services.
